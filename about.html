Department Profile

Electronics
Engineering

Specialization in VLSI Design & Technology — CSMSS College of Engineering, Kanchanwadi, Maharashtra.

Vision

Where we are headed

To be a nationally recognized center of excellence in Electronics Engineering and VLSI technology, producing graduates who lead innovation in semiconductor design, embedded systems, and intelligent electronics.

Mission

What drives us

Provide rigorous, industry-aligned education in VLSI and electronics disciplines. Foster research, design thinking, and practical lab skills producing engineers with strong ethical values.

Specializations

Technical Focus Areas

⚡

VLSI Design

RTL design, synthesis, place & route, timing analysis using industry-standard EDA tools.

🔌

Embedded Systems

Microcontroller programming, RTOS, IoT device design, and hardware-software co-design.

〰️

Analog & Mixed Signal

Amplifier design, ADC/DAC, operational amplifiers, and mixed-signal circuit simulation.

📡

Signal Processing

DSP algorithms, filter design, MATLAB/Simulink analysis, and communication systems.

🧠

AI & Edge Computing

TinyML, edge AI deployment, neuromorphic chips, and AI-accelerator hardware design.

🔬

Research & Innovation

Student research projects, industry collaboration, paper publications & patent guidance.
